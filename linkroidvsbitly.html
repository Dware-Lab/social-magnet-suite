Choosing the right Saas

Linkroid vs. Bitly

Have you ever seen a comparison page where the “challenger” comes out better than the company writing the comparison?

Exactly. Neither have we. So when we set about comparing our CRM to others we wanted to take a different approach. Here’s the approach we’re taking:

  • The comparison we’ve put together is based on whether it’s possible (and how much it costs) to do in Bitly what you can do in linkroid.
  • We’re biased but we’re not blind: for some of you, Bitly will be the better option. What we’re trying to demonstrate is when linkroid is a better fit than our competitor.

Cliff Notes

When comparing these two products the main differences that stand out are features and cost. To get in Bitly Platinum what you get in linkroid, you have to integrate with 4 third party apps and pay individual costs associated with them. Additionally, there are some features like automatic historical import of your emails, auto-logging of conversations & automatic call forwarding that are just NOT possible in Bitly (with or without third party apps).

There is also a higher cost associated with Bitly. For the complete solution, Bitly costs $4766 with necessary third party apps, while linkroid costs $1359.
